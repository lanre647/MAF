About Mudashiru Ayanbisi Foundation

Mudashiru Ayanbisi foundation is a foundation in honour of Late Ayanbisi Mudashiru Omobolaji who was an asthmatic and a philantropist. The foundation focuses on orphans, widows and asthmatic patients. Mudashiru Ayanbisi Foundation would be visiting the needs/less privileged with less advantage in the society to proper care and nurture, the people who have families but live from hand to mouth as their income is even lesser than minimum wage of the society, the people who live in the ghetto.

Our Mission

At Mudashiru Ayanbisi Foundation (MAF), we are driven by a singular mission: to provide hope, support, and opportunities for orphaned children around the world. We believe every child deserves a loving home, a quality education, and the chance to pursue their dreams, regardless of their circumstances. We try our best to make a difference like Providing Shelter and Care, Access to Education, Healthcare and Nutrition, Family Reunification and Adoption...
